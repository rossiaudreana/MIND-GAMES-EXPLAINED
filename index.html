About me

I'm a Division 1 rower at the University of Miami and a 4x National Canadian Champion. My athletic journey taught me that success isn't just about what happens on the water—it's about building the mental resilience that carries you through everything.

For years, I've worked with sports psychologists to develop real mental training techniques. Not motivational Instagram quotes. Actual psychological tools that work when you're under pressure.

As a Mental Health Ambassador at Miami, I'm committed to breaking stigma around mental health in college athletics. I work with student-athletes to show them that seeking support isn't weakness—it's strategy.

I represent Canada on the national rowing team. I create honest content on TikTok about D1 life. And now I'm launching this podcast to share what I've learned with other athletes.
